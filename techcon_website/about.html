Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a grassroots gathering of tech enthusiasts, engineers, and innovators eager to share insights and explore emerging trends. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most anticipated technology conferences, drawing participants from around the globe.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to connect forward-thinking individuals and foster innovation through collaboration, education, and hands-on experiences. We strive to create an inclusive space where all voices in technology are heard and celebrated.
